
# 🏥 Maternal Healthcare Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes maternal healthcare data to understand the key factors influencing maternal health outcomes such as recovery, complications, and mortality.

Using Power BI, the raw hospital dataset was transformed into an interactive dashboard to identify patterns in antenatal care (ANC), delivery types, hospital stay duration, and regional healthcare performance.

The goal is to support data-driven decision-making in improving maternal healthcare services.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze maternal health records and uncover insights that help answer:

- What factors influence maternal outcomes?
- Does antenatal care (ANC) improve patient outcomes?
- How do delivery types affect complication risk?
- Are there regional disparities in healthcare performance?
- How does hospital stay vary across cases?

---

## 🧰 Tools Used

- Power BI (Dashboard Development)
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- DAX (KPI and Measures Creation)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Antenatal Care (ANC) Matters
Patients with higher ANC attendance show significantly better maternal outcomes compared to those with low or no attendance. This highlights the importance of early and consistent prenatal care.

---

### 2. Delivery Type Impacts Risk
Certain delivery types are associated with higher complication rates, indicating that some cases require closer medical attention and monitoring.

---

### 3. Regional Disparities Exist
There are clear differences in maternal health outcomes across regions, suggesting unequal access to quality healthcare services and resources.

---

### 4. Hospital Stay Reflects Case Complexity
Longer hospital stays are generally associated with more complicated cases or higher-risk deliveries.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

- Increase awareness and access to ANC services, especially in low-attendance regions
- Strengthen monitoring and care for high-risk delivery types
- Address regional gaps in healthcare access and service quality
- Improve post-delivery care processes to optimize hospital stay duration

---

## 📊 Impact

This dashboard provides healthcare stakeholders with a clear, data-driven view of maternal health performance. It enables:

- Early identification of high-risk patients
- Evaluation of antenatal care effectiveness
- Detection of regional inequalities
- Improved healthcare planning and resource allocation

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how raw healthcare data can be transformed into actionable insights using Power BI.

It highlights the importance of data analytics in improving maternal healthcare outcomes and supporting evidence-based decision-making in healthcare systems.
